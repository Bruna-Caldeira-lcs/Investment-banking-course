Financial Statement Analysis Exercise

Analyze real financial statements, calculate key ratios, and draw meaningful insights from financial data.

Learning Objectives

  • Interpret financial statements including income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements
  • Calculate and analyze key financial ratios to assess company performance
  • Identify financial trends and potential red flags
  • Compare financial metrics across industry peers
  • Develop insights and recommendations based on financial analysis

Exercise Materials

  • Apple Inc. Financial Statements (Excel)
  • Financial Ratio Analysis Guide (PDF)
  • Financial Analysis Template (Excel)

Instructions

Part 1: Financial Statement Review (30 minutes)

Review Apple Inc.'s financial statements for the past three fiscal years. Familiarize yourself with the company's income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement. Take notes on any significant changes or trends you observe.

Part 2: Ratio Analysis (60 minutes)

Using the provided Financial Analysis Template, calculate the following ratios for each of the three years:

  • Profitability Ratios: Gross Margin, Operating Margin, Net Profit Margin, ROA, ROE
  • Liquidity Ratios: Current Ratio, Quick Ratio, Cash Ratio
  • Efficiency Ratios: Asset Turnover, Inventory Turnover, Receivables Turnover
  • Leverage Ratios: Debt-to-Equity, Debt-to-Assets, Interest Coverage
  • Valuation Ratios: P/E, P/S, EV/EBITDA

Part 3: Peer Comparison (45 minutes)

Compare Apple's financial ratios to those of its industry peers (provided in the Excel file). Identify areas where Apple outperforms or underperforms relative to its competitors.

Part 4: Analysis and Recommendations (45 minutes)

Based on your analysis, prepare a 1-2 page report that includes:

  • Summary of Apple's financial performance over the three-year period
  • Key strengths and weaknesses identified through ratio analysis
  • Comparison to industry peers
  • Recommendations for potential investors (buy, hold, or sell)
  • Areas for further investigation

Submission Guidelines

Submit the following items:

  1. Completed Financial Analysis Template with all calculated ratios
  2. 1-2 page analysis report in PDF format
  3. Any additional supporting calculations or notes
