Frequently Asked Questions

What prerequisites do I need for this course?

This course is designed for beginners with no prior investment banking experience. However, a basic understanding of finance, accounting, and economics concepts will be helpful. The course starts with fundamentals and progressively builds to more advanced topics.

How long does it take to complete the course?

The course is self-paced, so completion time varies based on your prior knowledge and time commitment. On average, students spend 2-3 months to complete all modules and exercises thoroughly. Each module requires approximately 5-10 hours to complete, including reading materials, watching videos, and completing exercises.

Are the exercises based on real-world scenarios?

Yes, all exercises and case studies are designed to simulate real-world investment banking scenarios and use actual company data (with some modifications for educational purposes). This approach helps you develop practical skills that directly translate to on-the-job performance.

Will this course help me prepare for investment banking interviews?

Absolutely! Module 8 is specifically dedicated to interview preparation, covering both technical and behavioral questions commonly asked in investment banking interviews. Additionally, the knowledge gained throughout the course will help you answer technical questions confidently.

Can I download the course materials for offline use?

Yes, all course materials, including module content, exercises, templates, and guides, are available for download in PDF or Excel format. This allows you to study offline and refer to the materials during your career.

How is this course different from other investment banking courses?

This course offers a comprehensive curriculum covering all aspects of investment banking, from fundamentals to advanced topics. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications through real-world exercises and case studies. The course is designed specifically for beginners aiming to secure internships at international banks.

Do you offer any certification upon completion?

Yes, upon completing all modules and passing the final assessment, you will receive a Certificate of Completion that you can add to your resume and LinkedIn profile to demonstrate your investment banking knowledge and skills.

Is there any support available if I have questions?

Yes, you can submit questions through the contact form on the website. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. Additionally, we have a comprehensive FAQ section and detailed explanations within the course materials to address common questions.
